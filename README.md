# Manufacturing-Analysis-Dashboard(v1.0 - Overview Tab)
A Power BI dashboard built to monitor and analyse manufacturing operations across production, quality, machine downtime, and inventory. Designed to support plant managers and operations teams in identifying bottlenecks, tracking KPIs against targets, and prioritising corrective actions.<br>
Data scope: Jan – Nov 2024 | Refreshed: 26 Nov 2024
Records processed: 10,000+ production, downtime, and stock records
# 🎯 Business Questions Answered
1. Which months had the largest gap between planned and actual production?<br>
2. Where is the defect rate trending — improving or worsening?<br>
3. Which machines are driving the most downtime cost?<br>
4. Which materials are at critical stockout risk and what is the total reorder exposure?<br>
5. Are back orders being driven by supply chain issues or production failures?<br>
